Establish Corporate Branding

Your logo will be placed on our website along with a link to your career portal for anyone to access. You will also receive promotional emails through our listserve to alert our members of upcoming events and opportunities. Content must be emailed at least a week in advance.

Receive an Invitation to Closing Banquet

This closed event will be to celebrate the accomplishments of our members and to remiscience the good times we had this year. You will also be featured in our slideset during the event. We welcome you to come network with students and enjoy an exciting night made possible by your support.

Lead a General Meeting

You will be given roughly an hour to present anything from corporate life to technological stack. Our only ask is please engage the students with stories or encounters at your work! We found that a personal touch garners a better audience and retains the attention of our members.

(Choose One)

Host a Programming Competition

A time where students can shine and showcase their ability to think outside the box. To be involed in this event, we invite you to write problems for the competition, if you would like, and provide prizes for the top three competitors. It would be best to send some engineers to clarify any questions and help out the students.

Direct a Technology Workshop

With a limit of one hour, you are welcome to demo a tool used in your workplace. Other options include discussing why the choices of technology, building a simple application, or even host an open panel on the technologies of your choice. If you do need members to follow along on their machines, please let us know before hand.

Tour of your Office Space (Austin only)

Your company is invited to host a tour of your office space to help educate our members on the environment and culture in your work place. This yields a more memorable experience and allows you to interact at a more personal level. This perk is limited to companies in the Austin area due to the scheduling conflicts among students.

Dine with Selected Students

Companies may choose a restaurant in the Austin area to host an intimate dinnner. Sponsors will then have the option to select 10-20 student attendees from among a group of ACM members who express interest in attending dinner. Companies should plan to send an appropriate number of engineers/recruiters based on attendendance.

Access our Resume Book

ACM resume book will be distributed to all Gold sponsors during the fall semester. ACM will send a link to Gold sponsors, which will allow access to the resume book. The link will expire at the end of the school year (May 2018).

Speak at the Semesterly Kickoff

The biggest event each semester with an averge attendance of 150, the kickof serves to introduce you and your company. You will have around 40 minutes to present. We suggestbringing swag and engineers to the event as we found those to be the most receptive to our members.

Send a Distinguished Speaker

Working together with the department and our officers, you may send a distinguished speaker from your organization. The topic should be computer science related. A past speaker we hosted was Marty Weiner, CTO of Reddit, who talked about building a startup from the ground up.
